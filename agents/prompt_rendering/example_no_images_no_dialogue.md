# Example — Two Characters, No Images, No Dialogue

This example demonstrates a fully rendered prompt with:
- Two characters
- One scene
- One style
- No image references
- No speech or thought bubbles

---

## STYLE

Core Style:
- Render domain: Semi-realistic illustration
- Genre influences: slice of life, urban

Line & Detail:
- Line weight: clean, medium
- Detail level: moderate

Light Treatment:
- Lighting style: soft, even light

Color:
- Palette: muted, natural tones
- Saturation: medium-low

Rendering Technique:
- Painterly digital rendering
- Subtle texture, no heavy grain

Composition Bias:
- Naturalistic framing
- Balanced compositions

Mood & Atmosphere:
- Calm
- Grounded
- Observational

- Environment scale: medium
- Geometry: long street corridor
- Key landmarks: storefront windows, streetlight
- Anchor prop: metal street sign

Lighting:
- Time of day: afternoon

- Light direction: side-left
- Light temperature: neutral
- Shadow style: soft shadows
- Lighting intensity: medium

Atmosphere:
- Weather: clear

- Dominant colors: concrete gray, warm beige
- Accent colors: muted blue

Scene Lore:
- Quiet neighborhood connecting residential and commercial areas

---

## TASK

Render a single frame.

- Sally is walking along the sidewalk at a relaxed pace.
- John is walking beside Sally, slightly behind her.
- Both characters are moving in the same direction down the street.
- Maintain a natural distance between the characters.
- Do not change character appearances, clothing, or environment.
